Mini SOC Lab

I created a Mini SOC Lab to simulate cyber attacks and analyze them using Wazuh and custom SIEM rules. This hands-on project allowed me to understand the full workflow of threat detection, attack reproduction, and incident reporting in a safe lab environment.
